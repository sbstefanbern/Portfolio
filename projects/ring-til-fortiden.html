Ring til Fortiden

Concept project for an installation on Fredericia Vold

ABOUT THE PROJECT

'Ring til Fortiden' is the name off a full concept installation for Fredericia Vold in Fredericia, Denmark. They requested a solution which should aim to bring more visitors to the main attraction in town. This solution involves kids in an engaging and fun way, bringing them in contact with the soldiers and heroes of the city's past and sends them on missions to find lost soldiers throughout Fredericia Vold.
