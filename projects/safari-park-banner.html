Safari Park Banner

Concept banner for promoting a safari park

ABOUT THE PROJECT

With the goal of promoting a safari park, this banner features an overall picture composed of a total of four different images. Using a variety of lighting techniques and manual shading work, it was made to look like a dramatic realistic picture which aims to draw the attention of the viewer. This banner was made for my image manipulation final exam in my Graphic Design education. It was never used outside this setting.
